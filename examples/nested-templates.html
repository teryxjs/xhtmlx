Nested Templates

Users → Posts → Comments. Three levels of nested API calls demonstrating template composition and $parent access.

How it works

<!-- Level 1: Load users -->
<div xh-get="/api/users" xh-trigger="load">
  <template>
    <div xh-each="users">
      <span xh-text="name"></span>

      <!-- Level 2: Load posts per user -->
      <div xh-get="/api/users/{{id}}/posts"
           xh-trigger="load"
           xh-template="/templates/post-list.html">
      </div>
    </div>
  </template>
</div>

<!-- post-list.html: Level 3 loads comments -->
<div xh-each="posts">
  <h4 xh-text="title"></h4>
  <div xh-get="/api/posts/{{id}}/comments"
       xh-trigger="load"
       xh-template="/templates/comment-list.html">
  </div>
</div>
